Welcome to Bluemeteor AI Force

This workspace gives your team governed access to specialized internal agents: chat, artifacts, browser and tests where enabled, approvals for risky steps, and full auditability. The pilot is a controlled rollout to prove value, safety, and readiness before wider adoption.

Pilot goals

  • Validate real workflows per role (engineering, QA, PM, docs, data, support, DevOps).
  • Measure usefulness, time saved, and friction via structured feedback.
  • Confirm readiness signals (health, RBAC, evaluations, ops visibility) stay green.
  • Decide expand, fix, or pause using exit criteria in the success metrics doc.

Available agents (priority)

Fronto, Backo, Testo, Producto, Doco, Dato, Supporto, DevOpsy — each has a short guide with sample prompts on the Agent guides page.

Safe usage rules

  • No secrets or production credentials in prompts.
  • Review every artifact before merge or customer send.
  • Use approvals and internal policies for anything that could change systems or data.
  • Prefer local/staging targets for browser and tests unless explicitly approved.

How to start

  1. Complete onboarding for your role.
  2. Open your primary agent and run one sample prompt.
  3. Review outputs and submit feedback so we can improve.

How feedback will be used

Feedback is stored internally, aggregated on the Metrics page (admins), and summarized in pilot reports. It drives prompt, workflow, and readiness improvements — not individual performance reviews.

Support process

Use the Support page for escalation templates and what to attach (agent, prompt, run ID, trace ID). Critical security issues should be reported immediately to your admin contact.

Pilot timeline & success criteria

Follow your team’s dated plan in docs/internal-pilot-launch-plan.md. Success criteria and exit gates are defined in docs/pilot-success-metrics.md.
